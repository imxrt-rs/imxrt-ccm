pub unsafe fn frequency() -> (ARMClock, IPGClock)

Returns the ARM and IPG clock frequencies

The function assumes that the ARM clock runs on PLL1. The clock values may be incorrect until after the first call to set_frequency.

Safety

Reads multiple CCM registers without synchronization. It's safer to use Handle::frequency_arm to read the frequencies.
